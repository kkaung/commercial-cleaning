## Our Carpet Cleaning Process

At RZ Clean Sydney, we follow a detailed and systematic carpet cleaning process to ensure exceptional results and the highest level of cleanliness. Our team is trained to adhere to the highest standards of safety and efficiency, using only eco-friendly and high-quality cleaning products.

1. **Pre-Inspection**: Before we begin the cleaning process, our team conducts a thorough inspection of your carpets, identifying any areas of concern, such as heavily soiled or stained areas, and noting any specific cleaning instructions or preferences you may have.
2. **Carpet Preparation**: We carefully move and protect any furniture or other items in the area to be cleaned, ensuring a safe and efficient workspace for our technicians.
3. **Soil and Stain Pre-Treatment**: Our team applies specialized pre-treatment solutions to target stubborn stains, ground-in dirt, and other tough spots, allowing the cleaning agents to penetrate and break down the soiling before the main cleaning process begins.
4. **Hot Water Extraction**: Using state-of-the-art truck-mounted or portable hot water extraction equipment, our technicians thoroughly clean your carpets, using the combined power of hot water, specialized cleaning solutions, and powerful suction to remove deeply embedded dirt, grime, and allergens.
5. **Spot and Stain Removal**: During the cleaning process, our team pays special attention to any remaining spots or stains, applying targeted spot removal techniques and solutions to ensure a flawless finish.
6. **Deodorizing and Sanitizing**: After the cleaning process is complete, we apply eco-friendly deodorizers and sanitizers to leave your carpets smelling fresh and eliminating any lingering odors or bacteria.
7. **Drying and Final Inspection**: Our team uses specialized drying equipment to ensure your carpets are thoroughly dried, preventing the growth of mold or mildew. Once the carpets are dry, we conduct a final inspection to ensure that our high standards have been met and that you are completely satisfied with the results.
